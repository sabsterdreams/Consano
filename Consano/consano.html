Our Mission

'To raise awareness to and validate the unseen emotional pain young girls

experience in friendships and provide tools for healing,

self-protection, and long-term emotional growth.’

Our website seeks to help:

  • Girls experiencing friendship problems that seem more like bullying and emotional abuse
  • Girls wanting to learn more about signs of unhealthy behaviour in relationships
  • Parents, adults and teachers looking for educational resources and a safe space for girls to
    deepen their understanding on these very serious behaviours without them getting sucked
    into social media algorithms or chatting to strangers on forums.
    • Dealing with covert bullying should be less about investigating what happened and more about inviting the victim to receive care and heal. This builds their strength, tools, and confidence, helping them prevent future incidents by becoming more resilient.

    Whilst these resources are not a substitute for professional therapy, the validation and learning they provide serves as a way for victims to feel seen and supported.
